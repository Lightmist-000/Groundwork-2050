# The Groundwork

> Digging into the groundwork of the human mind — a research project on psychology, behaviour, and what shapes us.

---

## About

This project explores the psychology and behaviour behind how humans think, feel, and act. It is a personal research and writing initiative aimed at building a deeper understanding of the mind — from everyday patterns to the forces that drive human decision-making.

---

## Goals

- Explore key concepts in psychology and behavioural science
- Document research notes, insights, and reflections
- Develop original writing grounded in research
- Build a reference library of sources and readings

---

## Questions Being Explored

- What drives human behaviour beneath the surface?
- How do patterns form — and how are they broken?
- What shapes the way we think, react, and relate?
